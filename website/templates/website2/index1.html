Common Questions

What is DocusAI?

DocusAI is an enterprise AI health platform serving patients, clinicians, and laboratories offering deeply integrated clinical modules and secure data interpretation.

Can DocusAI replace a licensed provider?

No. DocusAI is an advanced clinical support tool. It provides analytical insights to augment proactive health management but does not replace professional diagnosis.

How secure is the platform?

Security is our core metric. Infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and stringent global privacy frameworks.

Do you offer enterprise APIs?

Yes, we provide robust GraphQL and REST APIs for seamless EHR/EMR integration. Contact our sales team for documentation.
